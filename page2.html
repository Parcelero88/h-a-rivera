About Us...

Hi, I'm Hector...

. . . and this is my crew.

I am a husband, father, and veteran. I am a native of Puerto Rico and joined the US Army in 1993, serving on active duty for 26 years. After retirement, my family and I moved to San Antonio, Texas looking for a place to finally grow roots. This is the space where I will tell you what new things I'm trying and how they worked out for me. It will be a theme-less journey because I now have the ability and flexibility to do pretty much anything. Having served for as long as I did, I am in tune with the challenges faced by both the service member and their immediate family. I hope to share what has worked of us and help your transition go smoother than mine.

You can reach me at info@senseandedibility.com!

Glad you are here to share in the adventures!
